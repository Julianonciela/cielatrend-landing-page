About CielaTrend

Founded in May 2024 by Juliano and Yecenia Incorvaia, CielaTrend embodies a unique vision combining international expertise and entrepreneurial innovation.

Our mission is threefold: to offer world-class consulting services, create accessible business opportunities for all, and provide our customers with a rewarding shopping experience as valued members of our community.

Inspired by our daughter Ciela, we aspire to build a future where global expertise, local entrepreneurship, and customer satisfaction meet to create unprecedented opportunities for everyone involved with CielaTrend.
